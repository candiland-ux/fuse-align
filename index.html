Week 1 — Establish Baseline Transparency
Share one account summary
Share a single bank or investment account summary. No judgment, no discussion — just visibility.
Week 2 — Expand Access
Discuss what financial transparency means to each of you
Not what you want from each other — what the word itself means to you, and why it matters.
Week 3 — Shared Access Pilot
Try shared view on primary account for one week
Not shared control — shared visibility. Notice what anxiety or relief comes up for each of you.
Week 4 — Reflect & Decide
Check in: has anxiety decreased? Has trust increased?
